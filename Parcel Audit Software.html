Enterprise parcel networks generate thousands of invoices per week — dimensional weight errors, zone misclassifications, and accessorial overcharges across every carrier. Sampling-based audit catches less than half. Overcharges compound silently while teams stay blind.

03

Manual Dispute Management

The Solution

What Freehand Does Across the Parcel Audit Lifecycle

Freehand's AI Teams for Parcel Audit execute the full audit lifecycle autonomously — from invoice ingestion through charge validation, dispute filing, and spend reporting. Each agent owns a distinct step so nothing is sampled, nothing is missed, and no overcharge goes unrecovered.

→

100% Invoice Audit Coverage

Every parcel invoice is audited against contracted rates, carrier tariffs, and shipment records — not a sample. Dimensional weight errors, zone misclassifications, duplicate charges, and service failures are flagged automatically, every cycle.

→

Automated Charge Validation

The Audit Agent validates every line item — base rate, fuel surcharge, dimensional weight, zone, accessorial — against contracted rates and shipment data. Discrepancies are flagged and categorized before payment is released.

→

Automated Dispute Filing

The Dispute Management Agent files claims with carriers automatically — with matched shipment evidence, within carrier dispute windows, across every carrier in the network. No manual claim prep. No missed recovery windows.

→

Parcel Spend Intelligence

The Spend Intelligence Agent classifies every charge by carrier, zone, service level, and charge type — giving supply chain and finance a single, queryable view of parcel cost across the entire network.

→

Carrier Performance Benchmarking

Every audit cycle generates carrier-level performance data: overcharge rate by carrier, charge type, and lane. Supply chain leaders use it to hold carriers accountable and negotiate from a position of evidence.

→

Continuous Monitoring & Anomaly Detection

The Anomaly Detection Agent flags billing pattern changes, carrier-level overcharge spikes, and duplicate invoice submissions before payment — providing a continuous signal layer on top of every audit cycle.

Technology

Powered by the Freehand Context Graph

Context is king. AI with context eliminates work.

Freehand's proprietary Context Graph unifies contracted rates, carrier tariffs, shipment data, accessorial rules, and audit records into a single semantic layer, enabling AI agents to validate charges correctly, file disputes accurately, and report spend with a complete audit trail.

The platform is built on the Freehand Logistics Language Model, a domain-specific LLM trained on parcel data, carrier rate structures, and logistics invoice patterns. This is what separates Freehand from generic AI tools applied to parcel data: the model understands how parcel invoicing works, carrier by carrier, charge type by charge type.

Every audit decision is grounded in verified contract rates and shipment data, not pattern matching on unstructured text.

Every dispute is fully traceable, from invoice through audit finding through claim resolution, with a complete audit record.

The Context Graph learns from every audit cycle. Charge validation accuracy and dispute win rates improve continuously, without manual rules updates.
